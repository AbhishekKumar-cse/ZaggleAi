# **App Name**: CFO-OS by ZaggLeAI

## Core Features:

- User and Role-Based Access Control: Secure login via Firebase Auth with Google OAuth or Email/Password, implementing role-based access for CFO, Finance Analyst, and Auditor with protected routes.
- Executive Command Center with AI Insights: A real-time CFO dashboard featuring dynamic KPIs, live spend heatmaps, an AI-generated 'CFO Morning Brief' streamed character-by-character, and an activity feed for AI agents.
- AI-Powered Spend Management: Real-time transaction tables with advanced filtering and sorting, spend analytics visualizations, an agentic AI for identifying anomalies, policy violations, and providing optimization recommendations.
- Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) Engine: Dashboards for budget vs. actuals, AI explanations for variance analysis, an AI-generated predictive forecasting module with scenario modeling, and a planning narrative tool.
- Intelligent Compliance & Audit Automation: AI-driven anomaly detection for transactions, an auto-reconciliation module, immutable compliance audit logs, and a policy management console with AI-suggested rules.
- Multi-Agent AI Orchestration Console: The central hub for viewing and dispatching tasks to autonomous finance AI agents in natural language, observing their real-time activity, and utilizing a persistent, context-aware AI chat interface tool.
- AI-Generated Financial Reports & Intelligence: Generate comprehensive financial reports with AI-narrated summaries for monthly CFO insights, spend analysis, cash flow, and compliance, with options for scheduling and export.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: Electric Teal (#00D4B8), a vibrant and precise hue reflecting digital innovation, perfectly contrasted against the dark scheme to highlight key data and interactive elements.
- Background color: Deep Obsidian (#0A0E1A), forming a powerful, immersive, and dark canvas inspired by Bloomberg Terminal aesthetics, ensuring high contrast for information density.
- Accent and alert color: Striking Amber (#F59E0B), reserved for crucial alerts, warnings, and highlighting actionable insights, providing a stark but complementary contrast to the primary teal.
- Headline and display text: 'Syne' (sans-serif), chosen for its geometric, distinctive character, reinforcing a modern and sophisticated aesthetic.
- Body text and general interface: 'IBM Plex Mono' (monospace), for a data-dense, technical, and highly legible presentation. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Numerical data and financial metrics: 'JetBrains Mono' (monospace), ensuring clarity and precision for all quantitative displays. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Sharp, modern, and geometric icons from Lucide React to define each module and action, ensuring immediate recognition and alignment with the fintech theme.
- A sharp, geometric layout featuring data-dense dashboards, a collapsible left sidebar navigation, and consistently designed cards with subtle borders and glow effects for a premium feel.
- Fluid and intentional animations including fade and slide page transitions, animated number counters, staggered chart entries, typewriter effect for AI responses, and subtle pulse effects for agent statuses, all enhancing real-time interactivity.
